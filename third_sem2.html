🧠 Anxiety Kill-Switch Protocol

When you feel "I should be studying X instead of Y" → Follow these steps:

  1. STOP. Close everything. Stand up.
  2. Deep breath × 3. Inhale 4 seconds, hold 4, exhale 4.
  3. Open this tracker. What does the schedule say?
  4. Trust the system. Say out loud: "The system knows better than my anxiety."
  5. Start with 5 minutes. Just 5 minutes on what the schedule says. Momentum kills anxiety.
